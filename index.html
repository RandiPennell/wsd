Pikachu Pokemon Black and White Version

Pikachu is a mouse Pokemon. He is 1'04" tall and weighs 13.2 pounds. It occasionally uses an electric shock to recharge a fellow Pikachu that is in a weakened state.

  • Pichu - Most basic form of Pikachu
  • Pikachu - Evolve from Pichu with high friendship
  • Raichu - Last evolved version of Pikachu
