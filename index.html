Pikachu is a mouse Pokemon. He is 1'04" tall and weighs 13.2 pounds. It occasionally uses an electric shock to recharge a fellow Pikachu that is in a weakened state. Pikachu is actually an evolved form of Pichu. However the most evolved form of Pikachu is Raichu. Pikachu is one of the most famous Pokemon characters, his Trainer is Ash.

Pikachu alone has the ability to release electric charges, out of electric sacs in its cheeks. This energy can be used in various ways in battle, but it is also used to roast Berries. According to the Pokedex, Pikachu has several special moves.

Pikachu's Special Moves

Some of the most common are listed below.

  • Discharge
  • Electro Ball
  • Growl
  • Thunder
  • Thunderbolt
  • ThunderShock
  • Thunder Wave
  • Tail Whip
  • Quick Attack
